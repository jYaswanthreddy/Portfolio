LegalBERT Model
Python BERT NLP

LegalBERT is an advanced legal analytics project that predicts case outcomes by combining deep learning and structured data. The model processes the 'facts' column from legal case documents using a BERT-based transformer to generate contextual embeddings, which are then merged with other case features. The workflow includes:

  • Comprehensive data preprocessing and handling of missing values
  • Extraction of BERT embeddings for legal text
  • Feature engineering and integration with structured data
  • Model training and evaluation using Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVM, XGBoost, and Neural Networks
The project demonstrates the power of transformer models in the legal domain, achieving robust performance in predicting whether the first party wins a case. It highlights the importance of combining textual and tabular data for real-world legal analytics and provides a reproducible pipeline for future legal AI applications.
